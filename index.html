About Me

Welcome to Jo Smith Photography! Based in Dundee, I am a passionate photography graduate who has spent my entire life in this beautiful city. I studied photography at Dundee university, and my deep connection to the local area and Scotland as a whole is reflected in my work.



My specialty lies in capturing the raw beauty of nature, but I also apply my skills to a wide range of photography genres to provide you with stunning, memorable images for any occasion. With a blend of technical expertise and an artist's eye, I strive to create photographs that evoke emotion and tell a story.

Jo Smith — Professional Photographer
